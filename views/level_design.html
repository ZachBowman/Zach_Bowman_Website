Level Design
As an experienced mapmaker, I've worked on dozens of new maps and environments for various games, including Doom, Quake 2, Quake 3, Half-Life, and Team Fortress Classic. My work has included single player, deathmatch, and team-based multiplayer maps in varied settings and art styles. I've displayed here some of my more recent work that best showcases my talents.



Evolver Source is a partial conversion of Half-Life 2 into an area from Evolver, a future game idea we've been working on. For this project I've created not only the geometry but all of the wall textures myself. I'm producing the content on a modular basis and the next add-ons planned are for a new weapon design and a new creature.


The Arena is a gladiator-style pit used for bloody deathmatches. Each combatant spawns into a holding cell with one weapon and select items for balance. A switch in the cell raises the gate to the arena, and begins a fight for survival.


The Sewage Treatment Plant emphasizes stealth and strategy over typical running-and-shooting deathmatch tactics. The map has lots of armor and mostly light-to-medium weapons that make one-shot kills difficult, and features plenty of small hiding spots and tight corners for ambushes and quick escapes. For those who explore there's a quad damage hidden in a pipe near the top of the building, but they'll have to fall three floors into a vat of raw sewage to get it.
