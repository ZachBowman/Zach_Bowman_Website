Mapping


As an experienced mapmaker, I've created over one-hundred new maps and environments for various games, including Doom, Doom 2, Quake 2, Quake 3, Half-Life, and Team Fortress Classic. My work has included single player, deathmatch, and team-based multiplayer maps in various time and place settings and art styles. I've displayed here some of my more recent work that best showcases my talents.
Oblivion
Oblivion Evolved is a partial conversion of The Elder Scrolls IV into another game idea I've been working on. For this project I've created not only the geometry but all of the wall textures myself. Because of the nature of the game engine, I've also had to construct the architecture from prefabricated pieces I molded in 3DS Max.
Quake 3


The Arena is a gladiator-style pit used for bloody deathmatches. Each combatant spawns into a holding cell with one weapon and select items for balance. A switch in the cell raises the gate to the arena, and begins a fight for survival.
The Sewage Treatment Plant emphasizes stealth and strategy over typical running-and-shooting deathmatch tactics. The map has lots of armor and mostly light-to-medium weapons that make one-shot kills difficult, and features plenty of small hiding spots and tight corners for ambushes and quick escapes. For those who explore there's a quad damage hidden in a pipe near the top of the building, but they'll have to fall three floors into a vat of raw sewage to get it.
